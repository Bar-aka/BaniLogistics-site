Privacy Policy

This privacy page explains how website form information may be collected and used by Bani Global Logistics Limited.

1. Information Collected

We may collect details submitted through quote requests, tracking forms, and direct contact communication, including names, emails, phone numbers, origin and destination details, and shipment information.

2. How Information Is Used

Submitted information may be used to respond to quote requests, provide shipment support, improve customer communication, and manage logistics operations.

3. Data Protection

We aim to handle customer information responsibly and restrict access to relevant operational or administrative use.

4. Third-Party Services

Where necessary, information may be shared with service providers, carriers, clearance agents, or partners involved in shipment processing.

5. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be revised from time to time to reflect changes in legal, operational, or service requirements.
