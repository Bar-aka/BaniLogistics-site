Sourcing Quote

Use this when you want us to help source the goods before shipping. Share what you need and we will advise on the key specifications required to proceed.

What helps us quote faster for shipping

  • Preferred freight mode if known
  • Pickup city and final delivery point
  • Approximate cargo weight or volume
  • Commercial invoice or cargo description
  • Required delivery timeline

What we need for sourcing requests

  • Clear item name, model, or technical specification
  • Quantity and expected quality standard
  • Budget or target landed cost if available
  • Preferred supplier market or country
  • Links, photos, drawings, or brand references where possible
